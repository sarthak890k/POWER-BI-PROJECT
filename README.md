# Sales And Customer Dashboard

# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze coffee shop sales performance across different stores, products, and time periods. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The project demonstrates end-to-end Business Intelligence skills, including data cleaning, data modeling, DAX calculations, visualization design, and business analysis.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query 
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset
* Data modeling


---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains coffee shop transaction records, including:

* order id
* order Date
* Store Location
* Product Category
* Product Type
* Quantity Sold
* Unit Price
* Total Sales

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance.
* Track revenue and sales trends.
* Compare store performance.
* Identify top-selling products and categories.
* Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Orders, Total Quantity Sold, Average rating)
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Daily Sales Analysis
* Sales by Product Category
* Top Selling Products
* Store-wise Revenue Analysis
* Peak Business Hours Analysis
* Interactive Filters and Slicers
* Dynamic Visualizations

## 📌 Key Insights

* Coffee products generated the highest revenue.
* Morning hours experienced the highest customer traffic.
* A few products contributed significantly to total revenue.
* Some store locations consistently outperformed others.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Maintain sufficient inventory for high-demand products to prevent stock shortages.
2. Increase staffing during peak business hours to improve customer service.
3. Introduce promotional offers for low-performing products to increase sales.
4. Expand the best-selling product categories based on customer demand.
5. Use sales trends to improve inventory planning and seasonal forecasting.
